About CivicTrust

Empowering citizens with facts, not propaganda. We're building a more transparent and accountable society through technology and community engagement.

Our Story

CivicTrust was born from a simple yet powerful idea: in the age of misinformation, citizens deserve a reliable platform to verify claims, report issues, and engage in meaningful civic discourse.

Founded in 2024, we've combined cutting-edge AI technology with journalistic expertise to create a platform that serves as a beacon of truth in public discourse. Our AI-powered fact-checking system works alongside experienced journalists to provide rapid, accurate verification of public claims.

Today, we're proud to serve thousands of citizens, helping them make informed decisions and actively participate in their communities' governance.
